Squaritone Pianophone

Squaritone Pianophone is an experimental prog rock holiday-themed band that features two baritone saxophones and a piano. They make music or something.

Meet the Members!

Reb Mendoza

“The piano is not a table. It is my table.”

Mastermind behind the keys, Reb Mendoza is the composer of the band`s hit debut single “Duck Song,” and continued the proof of his mastery with “Duck Song II,” the title track on the new platinum-selling album. He enjoys long walks on the beach, poker, and making timelapses during class.

Ziaire Sherman

"Yo."

Head Bari Sax player of Squaritone Pianophone, Ziaire Sherman is literally too cool for this shit. Like, kind of a legend on the sax not gonna lie. Because he deemed being god on one instrument not enough, he also plays drum kit, clarinet, and steel drums. He`s responsible for the square wave-based genius featured in the “Phase Cancellation” EP. He chooses to live by a simple life motto and encourages the audience to communicate and not be stupid. Thanks.

Lilly Paul

“It`s not moral support if you don`t have morals.”

As the Secondary Bari Sax player and Local Cripple, Lilly Paul spends more time on the production end of things than the playing end. She enjoys pushing faders that change nothing about the sound to the untrained ear more than she does going “toot toot,” which, mad respect. “Zero Never Pi” is the song she`s most proud of that they`ve released.

Discography

“Duck Song”
Debut single released in October of 2020, sat at the top of Billboard Top 100 for over seven weeks, and was sampled on David Guetta`s latest record. We sued him for that.
“Phase Cancellation”
The band's first EP released in March of 2021, sold over 100,000 copies within the first month of release. Lineup of five tracks that are absolute bangers only. “Zero Never Pi” was featured in the end credit scene of Avengers 4: Tokyo Drift when Ludacris backed out of using his latest single.
“Duck Song II”
First studio album released in August of 2022, Squaritone Pianophone proved that they hadn`t lost their touch when the record reached platinum status within six days of the much-anticipated launch. The title track, “Duck Song II” became a worldwide phenomenon when it was played at the Queen of England`s funeral.
